The Best Movies According to Anamika

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Despicable Me

Despicable Me is a 2010 American 3D computer-animated comedy film produced by Illumination Entertainment. The film follows supervillain Gru (Carell) as he plans to steal the Moon, but his life changes when he meets three orphan girls and decides to adopt them.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

It is a 2001 fantasy film and based on the novel by J.K. Rowling. The film follows Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is famous in the wizarding world for surviving an attack by the dark wizard Voldemort as a baby.

Frozen

Frozen is a 2013 American 3D computer-animated musical fantasy film. It is based on Hans Christian Andersen's fairy tale "The Snow Queen". The story follows Princess Anna as she teams up with an iceman named Kristoff and his reindeer Sven to find her sister Elsa, whose icy powers have trapped their kingdom in eternal winter.
